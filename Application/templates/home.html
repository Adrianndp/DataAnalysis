Indicators

Economic indicators are some of the most valuable tools investors can place in their arsenals. In the selection Graphs you will see 2 of the most famous ones. RSI: is a momentum indicator used in technical analysis that measures the magnitude of recent price changes to evaluate overbought or oversold conditions in the price of a stock or other asset. The RSI is displayed as an oscillator (a line graph that moves between two extremes) and can have a reading from 0 to 100 and our other indicator is the EMA. EMA: The exponential moving average (EMA) is a technical chart indicator that tracks the price of an investment (like a stock or commodity) over time.
